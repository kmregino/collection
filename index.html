Minecraft flowers

Allium is found in flower forest biomes. It produces magenta dye.

Blue orchids are found in swamp and taiga biomes. It produces light blue dye.

Cornflowers are found in plains and flower forest biomes. It produces blue dye.

Dandelions grow in almost all Minecraft biomes. It produces yellow dye.

Lilacs are found in forest biomes. It produces magenta dye.

Lily of the valley flowers are found in forest biomes. It produces white dye.

Peonies are found in forest biomes. They produce pink dye.

Poppies can be found in most biomes. It produces red dye.

Rose bushes are found in forest biomes. They produce red dye.
